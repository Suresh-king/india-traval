Best Tour Destination of India

Agra

Agra is home to the beautiful Taj Mahal. On your visit to Agra, also visit Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri and Sikandra.

Alleppey

Alleppey is the hub for backwater tourism in Kerala. House boats locally called “kettuvallam” are available on hire on a daily basis.

Amritsar

Amritsar, Punjab’s biggest city was the center of celebrations of the 400th anniversary (Parkash Utsav) of the installation of the Guru Granth Sahib.

Auli

Auli is a part of the Garhwal mountain range. It lies about 15 km/9 miles from Joshmath on the Badrinath Pilgrimage route just beyond Rishikesh.

Bikaner

Bikaner is a prominent city of Rajasthan and is in the heart of the Thar Desert. It is famed for its architectural excellence

Chattisgarh

Chattisgarh is the state in Central India, endowed with a rich cultural heritage and attractive natural diversit also known land of waterfalls

Chennai

Chennai, the first city of the south, and capital of Tamil Nadu widely known as Madras till 1996, has a rich tradition and civilisation

Chittorgarh

Lying on the Berach River, Chittaurgarh is a city and municipality in Rajasthan state of western India.

Corbett National Park

The oldest national park in India, established in 1936. Located in the Nainital district of Uttarakhand, it is India’s first national park

Darjeeling

Explore the enchanting beauty of Darjeeling with its breathtaking views, iconic toy train rides, lush tea gardens, and vibrant local culture.

Delhi

The capital city of India, Delhi, can be labeled as a cultural melting pot where diverse Indian cultures dwell together.

Gangtok

Gangtok is the beautiful capital city of Sikkim. A Buddhist haven, Gangtok is wreathed with fluffy clouds and surrounded by majestic snow-capped mountains.

Goa

Goa is popular world-over for its beach trance parties, especially on the Anjuna beach. The beach is a major attraction for vacationers

Gulmarg

A beautiful meadow nestled among the mountains of Kashmir is 50 km (30 miles) from Srinagar, at an altitude of 2730 meters (9000 feet).

Jaipur

Built by the warrior-astronomer Raja Sawai Jai Singh II in 1727. Jaipur is a charming place, which dazzles the eye of tourist

Jaisalmer

In Rajasthan’s Thar Desert, lies the golden city of Jaisalmer which reminds one excerpt from the Arabian Nights tales.

Jodhpur

Jodhpur Jodhpur, in Rajasthan, is known for its rich culture, history, and heritage. This popular tourist destination is also known as the Blue City

Kalimpong

Kalimpong is a bustling town in the northern part of West Bengal. Apart from being a busy business hub, educational institutions

Katra

Katra is a town situated in the Trikuta Mountains of the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. Starting point for Vaishno

Khajjiar

Khajjiar, known as the "Mini Switzerland of India," is a picturesque hill station in Himachal Pradesh

Khajuraho

The 9th to 12th century temples of Khajuraho is the evidences of the architectural geniuses during the times of Chandela dynasty.

Kochi

Formerly, Cochin, was the centre of Indian spice trade for many centuries, and was known to the Yavanas (Greeks) as well as Romans

Kolkata

Kolkata, the "City of Joy," is a vibrant blend of rich history, colonial architecture, cultural festivals, and a bustling artistic spirit.

Kovalam

A beach town by the Arabian Sea in Thiruvananthapuram city, Kerala, India . It is Kerala’s most famous tourist attraction, India’s most famous beach.
